# Festival of Learning 2026 - Call for Papers

**Dates:** June 29 – July 3, 2026
**Location:** Oakwood Premier Coex Center, Seoul, South Korea
**Website:** https://festival-of-learning-2026.info/

Four leading conferences in AI and education co-located in Seoul:
- AIED (Artificial Intelligence in Education)
- EDM (Educational Data Mining)
- L@S (Learning @ Scale)
- Contributing: ISLS, SoLAR/LAK

---

## AIED 2026 - 27th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education

**Theme:** "From Tools to Teammates: Human-AI Synergy for Augmented Learning"
**Website:** https://aied-conference.org/2026/call-for-paper
**Submission:** EasyChair
**Publication:** Springer LNAI/LNCS

### Key Dates

| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Abstracts Due | January 26, 2026 |
| Full Papers Due | February 2, 2026 |
| Notification | March 16, 2026 |
| Camera-Ready | March 23, 2026 |

### Tracks

1. **Technical Aspects of AIED**
   - AI/ML methods and architectures
   - Natural language processing for education
   - Intelligent tutoring systems
   - Adaptive learning technologies

2. **Human Aspects of AIED**
   - Learner modeling and cognition
   - Pedagogical approaches
   - Human-computer interaction in education
   - Teacher and instructor perspectives

3. **Societal Aspects of AIED**
   - Ethics in AI education
   - Equity and accessibility
   - Policy implications
   - Cultural considerations

### Paper Formats

| Format | Length | Presentation |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Full Paper | 14 pages (including references) | Long oral talk |
| Short Paper | 8 pages (including references) | Short oral talk |

### Requirements

- Springer LNAI/LNCS format (LaTeX or Word templates)
- Double-blind review (anonymized submissions)
- Abstract submission mandatory (no new submissions after abstract deadline)
- In-person presentation required
- One unique registration per accepted paper
- No downgrade path from main submissions to posters

### Review Criteria

- Relevance to AIED
- Novelty of contribution
- Technical soundness
- Significance of results
- Clarity of presentation

### Ethics Standards

Submissions should address:
- Demographic representation
- Sampling inclusivity
- Ethical considerations
- Potential impacts on diverse populations

## L@S 2026 - 13th ACM Learning @ Scale

**Website:** https://learningatscale.hosting.acm.org/las2026/call-for-papers/
**Submission:** EasyChair (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=las2026)
**Format:** ACM 2-column proceedings template

### Key Dates

| Submission Type | Deadline |
|-----------------|----------|
| Research Papers (abstract) | February 9, 2026 (11:59 PM AoE) |
| Research Papers (full) | February 16, 2026 (11:59 PM AoE) |
| Work-in-Progress | February 16, 2026 (11:59 PM AoE) |
| Demonstrations | April 14, 2026 (11:59 PM AoE) |

### Paper Types

| Type | Length | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Research Papers | Up to 10 pages (excl. refs) | Mature, complete research |
| Work-in-Progress | Up to 4 pages (excl. refs) | Early-stage or ongoing work |
| Demonstrations | Up to 2 pages (excl. refs) | System/tool demos |

### Topic Areas

- **Instruction at Scale** - How teachers scale instruction, effective strategies
- **Interventions at Scale** - Effects of interventions on student learning/performance
- **Generative AI for Learning** - Stakeholder experiences, student/teacher interactions with GenAI, potentials and limitations
- **Systems and Tools** - Research designing and developing systems supporting learning
- **Learner Behavior Modeling** - Analytics and behavioral analysis
- **Informal Online Learning** - Learning outside formal educational contexts
- **Literature Reviews** - Systematic reviews on learning at scale
- **Equity, Fairness, Bias** - Transparency in AI-assisted education
- **Accessibility** - Digital learning environment accessibility
- **Underrepresented Learners** - Systems serving diverse populations

### Requirements

- Double-blind peer review (anonymized)
- ACM 2-column proceedings template (Word or LaTeX)
- Original work not under review elsewhere
- Supporting materials in repositories (e.g., OSF.io)

## EDM 2026 - 19th International Conference on Educational Data Mining

**Theme:** "Educational Data Mining Across Borders: Bridging Disciplines, Contexts, and Perspectives"
**Website:** https://educationaldatamining.org/edm2026/call-for-papers/
**Submission:** EasyChair (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=edm2026)

### Key Dates

| Submission Type | Deadline |
|-----------------|----------|
| JEDM Journal Track | Nov 1, Dec 1, 2025; Jan 26, 2026 |
| Full/Short Paper Abstracts | February 2, 2026 |
| Full/Short/Industry Papers | February 9, 2026 |
| Workshops/Tutorials | February 9, 2026 |
| Posters/Demos/Doctoral Consortium | April 6, 2026 |
| Camera-Ready | April 30, 2026 |

All deadlines: 23:59 (11:59 PM) Anywhere on Earth

### Paper Types

| Type | Length | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Full Papers | 10 pages | Original, substantive, mature, unpublished work |
| Short Papers | 6 pages | Original work including early-stage projects |
| Industry Papers | 6 pages | Innovative EDM uses in commercial settings |
| JEDM Journal Track | Varies | Published in Journal of Educational Data Mining |
| Doctoral Consortium | 2-4 pages | Graduate research topic and contributions |
| Posters | 2-4 pages | Work-in-progress or last-minute results |
| Demos | 2-4 pages | EDM tools, systems, or educational systems using EDM |
| Workshops | 2-4 pages | Plan to conduct workshop and stimulate growth |
| Tutorials | 2-4 pages | Field/tool description with hands-on learning |

### Topic Areas

- **Data Mining Techniques** - Methods for educational research questions
- **Domain Knowledge Modeling** - Representing educational domains
- **Educational Recommenders** - Personalized learning systems
- **Equity, Privacy, Transparency, Fairness** - Ethical considerations in EDM
- **Learner Cognitive Modeling** - Understanding learner cognition
- **Learner Behavior Modeling** - Analyzing learner actions and patterns
- **Learning Analytics** - Measuring and analyzing learning data
- **Social and Collaborative Learning** - Group learning dynamics
- **Reproducibility and Benchmarking** - Replication and standards

### Data Sources

EDM welcomes research using data from:
- Learning management systems
- Interactive learning environments
- Intelligent tutoring systems
- Educational games
- Data-rich learning activities

### JEDM Journal Track Details

Papers submitted to the Journal of Educational Data Mining (JEDM) in the "EDM 2026 Journal Track" section and accepted before May 31, 2026 will be:
- Published in JEDM
- Presented during the conference

Three cut-off deadlines for review cycles: November 1, December 1, 2025, and January 26, 2026.
