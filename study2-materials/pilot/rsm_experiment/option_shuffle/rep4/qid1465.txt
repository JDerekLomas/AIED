**The Logic Behind the Errors:**

*   **Option D (4):** The "Ignoring BIDMAS" Trap. These students work strictly left-to-right: $3 - 5 = -2$, then $-2 + 4 = 2$, then $2 \times 2 = 4$. This is very common for students who haven't internalised priority of operations.
