*   **Option B (Correct):** To solve $3p = 1/12$, you must divide by 3 (or multiply by $1/3$), giving $p = 1/36$.
*   **Option C ($4$):** A "Below Basic" classic. They rearrange $3p = 1/12$ into $p = 12 \div 3$ as if the 12 were a whole number on the other side.
