**Reflections on the errors:**

*   **B (The 'Improper' Pitfall):** This is a very "attractive" wrong answer for proficient students. They instinctively convert to improper fractions to add ($11/5 + 7/5 = 18/5$) but forget to convert back to a mixed number as requested.
