Here is the prediction of student responses:

**below_basic** (Targeting Grade 1-2)
*Focus: Often forget the zero exists as a value and struggle with the division notation.*
* **A=20%**
* **B=30%** (Attracted to the horizontal layout; often miss the lack of brackets)

**basic** (Targeting Grade 3-4)
*Focus: Understand they need to add them all, but frequently ignore the 0 when counting the total numbers (denominator).*
