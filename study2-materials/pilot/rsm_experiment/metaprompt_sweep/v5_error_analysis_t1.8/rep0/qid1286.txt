*   **The Division Error (B):** Students correctly identify that $\pi$ should be on the bottom, but they "bring the fraction over" without flipping it, keeping 4 on top.
*   **The Power/Root Error (D):** Lower-ability students often fail to group the entire expression under the cube root, or they try to cube root $V$ first and then perform the multiplication/division separately.
*   **Proficient vs. Advanced:** Advanced students instinctively use the reciprocal $3V/4\pi$. Proficient students often write out $V \div 4/3$ and successfully reach $3V/4$.
