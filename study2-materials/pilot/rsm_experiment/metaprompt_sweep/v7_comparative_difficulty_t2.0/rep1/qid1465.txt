This is a classic "trap" question. To a seasoned teacher, this screams **Order of Operations (BIDMAS/BODMAS)** combined with **directed number (integers)**. 

While the individual components are taught early, putting them together in Year 9 creates a high "misconception activation." Many students follow BIDMAS like a rigid set of instructions (doing addition before subtraction) rather than treating addition and subtraction as equal priority from left-to-right.
