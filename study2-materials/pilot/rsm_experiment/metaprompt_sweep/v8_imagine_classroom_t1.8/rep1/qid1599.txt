The **Advanced** group (your Oscar and Maya types) will find this trivial. They treat the division sign and the fraction bar as synonymous. The only "danger" for them is overthinking whether they need to expand the denominator, which isn't an option here.

The **Proficient** group (the steady middle) will largely get it right, though a few like Leo might see the "c" and the "4" and reflexively think they need to do "something" to the numbers, drifting toward B.

The **Basic** group is where the "visual noise" of algebra takes over. They often struggle with the "order of operations" in a fraction. Many will pick **D** because they subconsciously prefer the heavier expression (h+4) to be on top, or they just get the divisor and dividend swapped.
