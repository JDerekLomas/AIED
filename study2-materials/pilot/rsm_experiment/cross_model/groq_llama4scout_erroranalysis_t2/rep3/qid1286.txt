## Step 1: Understand the formula for the volume of a sphere
The volume \(V\) of a sphere is given by the formula \(V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3\), where \(r\) is the radius of the sphere.

## 2: Rearrange the formula to solve for \(r\)
To find the radius \(r\), we rearrange the formula: \(r^3 = \frac{3V}{4\pi}\). Taking the cube root of both sides gives \(r = \sqrt[3]{\frac{3V}{4\pi}}\).
