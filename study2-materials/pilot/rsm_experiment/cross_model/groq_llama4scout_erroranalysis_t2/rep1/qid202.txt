## 2: Add the improper fractions
Adding these improper fractions: \( \frac{11}{5} + \frac{7}{5} = \frac{11 + 7}{5} = \frac{18}{5} \).

To convert \( \frac{18}{5} \) back into a mixed number: \( \frac{18}{5} = 3 \frac{3}{5} \) because \( 18 \div 5 = 3 \) remainder \( 3 \).
