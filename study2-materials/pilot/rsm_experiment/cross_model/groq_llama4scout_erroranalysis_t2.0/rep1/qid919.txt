## Step 1: Understanding the Question

## 2: Calculating the Correct Answer
To find the correct answer, substitute \(x = 0.3\) into the equation \(y = 3x^{2}\).
\[y = 3 \times (0.3)^{2} = 3 \times 0.09 = 0.27\]
So, the correct answer is B) \(0.27\).
