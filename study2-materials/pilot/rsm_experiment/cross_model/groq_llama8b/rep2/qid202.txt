To solve \( 2 \frac{1}{5}+1 \frac{2}{5}\), students should add the whole numbers 2 and 1 (which equals 3) and add the fractions \( \frac{1}{5}+   \frac{2}{5}\): 

\(    3 + (\frac{1}{5}  + \frac{2}{5} )   \)

Adding \( \frac{1}{5} \)  and \( \frac{2}{5} \) equals \( \frac{3}{5}\)

So, students then calculate:  \(    3 \frac{3}{5} \)
