The correct order of operations is often remembered by the acronym BIDMAS (Brackets, Indices, Division, Multiplication, Addition, and Subtraction). 1. Multiplication: \(4 \times 2 = 8\)
2. Then, addition and subtraction from left to right: \(3 - 5 + 8 = 3 - 5 = -2\), then \(-2 + 8 = 6\).

Another error could be not applying the multiplication first.
