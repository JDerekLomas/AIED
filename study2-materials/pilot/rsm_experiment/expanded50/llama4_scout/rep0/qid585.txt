\[4x+y=7\]
\[3x+2y=4\]

Multiplying the first equation by 2 gives:
\[8x + 2y = 14\]
Subtracting the second equation from this result:
\[8x + 2y - (3x + 2y) = 14 - 4\]
\[5x = 10\]
\[x = 2\]
