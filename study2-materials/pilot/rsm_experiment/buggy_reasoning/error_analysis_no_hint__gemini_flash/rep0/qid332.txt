In this Year 9 mock, this question specifically tests "order of operations in reverse" when solving linear equations. 

**Tom's method** follows the standard "Flowchart" or "Inverse Operations" approach: he tackles the subtraction first ($12 + 5 = 17$). This is what the vast majority of students are taught as the "safest" first step.

**Katie's method** is the "Eliminate the Denominator" approach. While mathematically valid, it is the number one "trap" for Year 9 students.
