## Step 1: Understand the equation and the task
The equation given is $10x - 6y = 30$. We need to find the value of $x$ when $y = 0.5$.

## 2: Solve the equation for $x$ when $y = 0.5$
Substitute $y = 0.5$ into the equation:
$10x - 6(0.5) = 30$.
This simplifies to:
$10x - 3 = 30$.
Adding 3 to both sides:
$10x = 33$.
Dividing both sides by 10:
$x = 3.3$.
