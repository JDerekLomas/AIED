## Step 1: Understand the equation and the task
The given equation is \(10x - 6y = 30\), and we need to find the value of \(x\) when \(y = 0.5\). To do this, we substitute \(y = 0.5\) into the equation and solve for \(x\).

## 2: Solve the equation for \(x\) when \(y = 0.5\)
Substituting \(y = 0.5\) into the equation gives us \(10x - 6(0.5) = 30\).
Simplifying, we get \(10x - 3 = 30\).
Adding 3 to both sides gives \(10x = 33\).
Dividing both sides by 10 gives \(x = 3.3\).

## 5: Analyze option C: \(3.3\)
