This question tests Order of Operations (BIDMAS/BODMAS). While the multiplication is relatively straightforward, the primary "trap" is the order of addition and subtraction. Many students mistakenly believe that Addition must come before Subtraction because 'A' precedes 'S' in the acronym, rather than treating them with equal priority from left to right.
