The task is to analyze a multiple-choice question about finding a missing value in a table representing a linear equation \(4x + 2y = 10\), and to predict the percentage of students at different ability levels (below basic, basic, proficient, advanced) who would choose each incorrect option.

## 2: Identifying the correct solution
To find the value that replaces the star (when \(x = 0\)), we substitute \(x = 0\) into the equation \(4x + 2y = 10\).
\[4(0) + 2y = 10\]
\[2y = 10\]
\[y = 5\]
So, the correct answer is \(C) 5\).
