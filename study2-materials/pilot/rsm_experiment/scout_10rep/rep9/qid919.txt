## Step 1: Understand the equation and the task
The equation given is \(y = 3x^2\). We need to find \(y\) when \(x = 0.3\).

## 2: Calculate the correct value of \(y\)
Substitute \(x = 0.3\) into the equation \(y = 3x^2\).
\[y = 3 \times (0.3)^2 = 3 \times 0.09 = 0.27\]
