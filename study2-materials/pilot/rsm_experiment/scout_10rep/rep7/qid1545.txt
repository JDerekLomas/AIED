## Step 1: Analyzing the Question
The question requires students to compare the values of two expressions: $8+12 \div 4$ and $(8+12) \div 4$. - Then addition: $8 + 3 = 11$

Next, evaluate $(8+12) \div 4$:
- Parentheses first: $8 + 12 = 20$
- Then division: $20 \div 4 = 5$
