## Step 1: Analyzing the question and answer choices
The question requires students to multiply out the brackets \((m-5)(m-7)\) using the FOIL method, which stands for First, Outer, Inner, Last. This method helps in remembering the steps to expand the product of two binomials.

## 2: Understanding the FOIL method application
Applying FOIL:
- First: \(m \times m = m^2\)
- Outer: \(m \times -7 = -7m\)
- Inner: \(-5 \times m = -5m\)
- Last: \(-5 \times -7 = 35\)

## 3: Combining like terms
After applying FOIL, we combine the like terms:
\(m^2 - 7m - 5m + 35 = m^2 - 12m + 35\)
