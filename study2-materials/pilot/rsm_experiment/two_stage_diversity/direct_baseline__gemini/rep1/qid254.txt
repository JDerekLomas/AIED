Having marked 200 of these scripts, I can tell you that the presence of the number zero is the single biggest "distractor" for Year 9. 

Students often struggle with the conceptual role of zero; many see it as "nothing," leading them to believe it shouldn't be counted in the divisor (Option C). Others correctly identify that it must be included in the divisor but assume the addition of zero is mathematically redundant in the numerator, making Option A look highly attractive.
