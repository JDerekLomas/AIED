The specific misconception or procedural error that a UK Year 9 student might apply in this context is the incorrect handling of negative numbers when raising them to an even power. A common error is to neglect that raising a negative number to an even power results in a positive value, specifically due to a mistaken application of the rule for odd powers where a negative base yields a negative result.
