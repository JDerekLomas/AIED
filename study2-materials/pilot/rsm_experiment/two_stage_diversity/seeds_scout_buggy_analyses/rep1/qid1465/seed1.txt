The specific misconception or procedural error that a UK Year 9 student might apply is ignoring the order of operations (PEMDAS/BODMAS), specifically performing addition and subtraction before multiplication. This is often referred to as not following the order of operations.

3. Then, multiply: \(4 \times 2 = 8\)
4. Finally, add the results: \(-2 + 8 = 6\)
