A student might incorrectly evaluate the expressions as follows:
- For \(8 + 12 \div 4\), they might calculate it directly without following BODMAS/PEMDAS: \(8 + 12 = 20\), then \(20 \div 4 = 5\).
- Alternatively, they might evaluate \((8 + 12) \div 4\) correctly using BODMAS/PEMDAS: \(8 + 12 = 20\), then \(20 \div 4 = 5\).
