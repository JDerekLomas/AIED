### The Specific Difficulty: The Temptation of Additive Operations on Denominators
A common conceptual hurdle for students is the tendency to apply a "blanket" additive rule to every digit they see in a fraction problem. While students may correctly add the whole numbers ($2+1=3$) and the numerators ($1+2=3$), many are drawn to adding the denominators ($5+5=10$) by sheer force of habit or perceived symmetry.
