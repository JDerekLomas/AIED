## 3: Calculate the correct mean

- For 5 spots: $5 \times 4 = 20$
- For 6 spots: $6 \times 9 = 54$
- For 7 spots: $7 \times 9 = 63$

Total spots = $20 + 54 + 63 + 24 = 161$
Total ladybirds = $4 + 9 + 9 + 3 = 25$
Mean spots per ladybird = $161 / 25 = 6.44$
