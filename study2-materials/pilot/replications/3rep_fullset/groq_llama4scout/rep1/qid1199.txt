To find the number of hours in 5 weeks, we need to know that there are 7 days in a week and 24 hours in a day. So, the correct calculation is \(5 \times 7 \times 24\), because you first find out how many days are in 5 weeks (\(5 \times 7\)) and then convert those days into hours (\( \times 24\)).
