## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question requires students to convert 10 litres into cubic metres. Knowing that 1 cubic metre equals 1000 litres is crucial for solving this problem.

## 2: Identifying the Correct Conversion
To convert litres to cubic metres, we use the conversion factor: 1 cubic metre = 1000 litres. Therefore, 10 litres is equal to \( \frac{10}{1000} = 0.01 \) cubic metres.
