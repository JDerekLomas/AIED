## Step 1: Understand the concept of triangle numbers
Triangle numbers are a sequence of numbers where each number can be represented as a triangle with dots. The nth triangle number is the number of dots in the triangle with n dots on a side, and it is given by the formula: \(T_n = \frac{n(n + 1)}{2}\). To find the next triangle number in a sequence, we need to understand the pattern or calculate the difference between consecutive numbers.

## 2: Identify the given triangle numbers and their positions

So, \(120 = T_{15}\) and \(136 = T_{16}\).

## 3: Calculate the next triangle number
Since \(136 = T_{16}\), the next triangle number, \(T_{17}\), can be calculated using the formula: \(T_{17} = \frac{17(17 + 1)}{2} = \frac{17 \times 18}{2} = 153\).
