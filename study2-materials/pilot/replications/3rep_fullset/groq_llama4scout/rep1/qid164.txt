## Step 1: Understanding the Question

## 2: Identifying the Formula for the Area of a Triangle
The formula for the area of a triangle when the base and height are known is \(\frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height}\). In this case, the base and height provided are 11cm and 8cm, respectively.

## 3: Calculating the Area
