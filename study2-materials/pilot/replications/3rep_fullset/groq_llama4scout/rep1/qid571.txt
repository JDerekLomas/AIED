Since 6 is greater than or equal to 10 (or more specifically, greater than 5 when considering rounding rules), we would round up to the next multiple of 20.

- The nearest higher multiple of 20 is 660.

Given that 646 is closer to 660 than to 640 (but still needs to be rounded to the nearest 20), we actually compare it directly to the multiples of 20:
- 646 - 640 = 6
- 660 - 646 = 14

Since 6 is less than 14, 646 rounded to the nearest 20 is 640.
