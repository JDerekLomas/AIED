## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question presents a fair four-sided spinner labeled with the numbers 1, 2, 3, and 4. The task is to find the probability of spinning a 2, expressed as a percentage.

## 2: Identifying the Correct Answer
Since the spinner is fair and has four sides, each side has an equal chance of landing face up. There is only one side labeled with a 2, so the probability of spinning a 2 is 1 out of 4, or 1/4.

## 3: Converting Probability to Percentage
To convert 1/4 into a percentage, we calculate (1/4) * 100 = 25%.
