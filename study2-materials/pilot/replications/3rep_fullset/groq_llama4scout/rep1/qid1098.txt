\[ \frac{3x+1}{3} \div \frac{9x^2-1}{12} = \frac{3x+1}{3} \times \frac{12}{9x^2-1} \]

\[ = \frac{3x+1}{3} \times \frac{12}{(3x-1)(3x+1)} \]
We can cancel \( (3x+1) \) from numerator and denominator:
\[ = \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{12}{3x-1} \]
\[ = \frac{12}{3(3x-1)} \]
