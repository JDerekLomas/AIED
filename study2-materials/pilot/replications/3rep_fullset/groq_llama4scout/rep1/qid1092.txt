## Step 1: Understanding the Concept of Median
The median of a set of numbers is the middle value when the numbers are arranged in ascending order. If there is an even number of observations, the median is the average of the two middle numbers.

## 2: Applying the Concept to the Given Numbers
The given numbers are \(2, 0.2, 0.02, 0.002\). Arranging them in ascending order gives \(0.002, 0.02, 0.2, 2\). Since there are four numbers (an even amount), the median will be the average of the two middle numbers, which are \(0.02\) and \(0.2\).

## 3: Calculating the Median
The median = \((0.02 + 0.2) / 2 = 0.22 / 2 = 0.11\).
