## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question asks students to round the number \(0.095643\) to \(1\) significant figure. Rounding to \(1\) significant figure involves looking at the first digit of the number and then deciding whether to round up or down based on the second digit. If the second digit is \(5\) or greater, we round up; if it's less than \(5\), we round down.
