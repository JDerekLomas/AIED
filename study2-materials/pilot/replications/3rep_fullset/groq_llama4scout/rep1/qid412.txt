## Step 1: Understanding the Probability Scale
The probability scale ranges from 0 to 1, where:
- \(0\) represents an impossible event,
- \(0.5\) represents an event that is equally likely to happen or not happen,
- \(1\) represents a certain event.

## 2: Interpreting the Given Probability
