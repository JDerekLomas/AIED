## 2: Identifying Key Properties of the Number 15
15 is an odd number because it cannot be divided evenly by 2. However, 15 is not a square number since there is no integer that, when multiplied by itself, equals 15.

## 3: Analyzing the Venn Diagram Regions
- Region A refers to numbers that are square numbers but not odd (implying they are even square numbers).
- Region B refers to numbers that are both square numbers and odd.
- Region C refers to numbers that are odd but not square numbers.
- Region D refers to numbers that are neither square numbers nor odd numbers.

## 4: Determining the Correct Region for 15
Since 15 is odd but not a square number (because 15 does not have an integer square root), it belongs to Region C.
