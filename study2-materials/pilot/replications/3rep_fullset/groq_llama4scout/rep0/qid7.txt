The question \( 43.2 \div 10 = \) is testing the understanding of what happens when you divide a number by 10. A common misconception is that students might think dividing by 10 involves subtracting 10 from the number or they might not understand how the decimal point moves.

## Step 1: Understanding the Operation

- Some students might confuse division by 10 with multiplication by 10 or subtraction/addition.
