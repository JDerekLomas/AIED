The question asks students to round the number \(2.506710\) to three significant figures. Rounding to significant figures involves looking at the digit immediately to the right of the last significant figure. If this digit is 5 or greater, the last significant figure is rounded up; if it's less than 5, the last significant figure remains unchanged.
