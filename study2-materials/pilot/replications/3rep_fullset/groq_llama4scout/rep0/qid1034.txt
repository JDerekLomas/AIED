## Step 1: Understanding the Misconception
A common misconception is that students might not accurately estimate the position of a number on a number line, especially if the scale is not well understood or if the number is close to a benchmark (like 1000, 500, etc.).

## 5: Analyzing Option D
Option D presents 8967 on a 0-10000 number line, after the midpoint. The midpoint is 5000, and 8967 is indeed after 5000, making this a reasonable estimation.
