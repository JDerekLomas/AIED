## Step 1: Understand the question and the concept of mean
The question asks for the mean of a given set of data: \(5, 2, 0, 2, 3\). The mean is calculated by summing all the numbers and then dividing by the count of the numbers. So, the calculation would be \((5 + 2 + 0 + 2 + 3) / 5\).

## 2: Calculate the mean
To find the mean, we first sum the numbers: \(5 + 2 + 0 + 2 + 3 = 12\). Then, we divide by the count of numbers, which is \(5\). So, \(12 / 5 = 2.4\).
