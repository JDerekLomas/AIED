## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question presents a table with the number of goals scored by a Hockey team and the frequency of each score. The task is to determine the total number of matches played by the team.

However, the question seems straightforward as it directly asks for the total number of matches, which can be found by summing up the frequencies.

## 3: Analyzing the Table
The table provides:
- 0 goals scored: 6 matches
- 1 goal scored: 7 matches
- 2 goals scored: 4 matches
- 3 goals scored: 3 matches
- 4 goals scored: 1 match

## 4: Calculating the Total Number of Matches
To find the total number of matches, we sum the frequencies:
6 (for 0 goals) + 7 (for 1 goal) + 4 (for 2 goals) + 3 (for 3 goals) + 1 (for 4 goals) = 21
