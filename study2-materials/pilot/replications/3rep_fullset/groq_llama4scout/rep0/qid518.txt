## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question presents a regular octagon with one of its interior angles labeled as \( x \). The task is to find the measure of angle \( x \).

## 2: Recall the Formula for Interior Angles of a Polygon
The sum of interior angles in any polygon can be found using the formula \( (n-2) \times 180^{\circ} \), where \( n \) is the number of sides of the polygon. For a regular polygon, since all angles are equal, the measure of each interior angle can be found by dividing the total sum of interior angles by the number of sides.

## 3: Applying the Formula to an Octagon
An octagon has 8 sides, so \( n = 8 \). The sum of the interior angles is \( (8-2) \times 180^{\circ} = 6 \times 180^{\circ} = 1080^{\circ} \). Since the octagon is regular, all interior angles are equal. Therefore, each interior angle, including \( x \), is \( \frac{1080^{\circ}}{8} = 135^{\circ} \).
