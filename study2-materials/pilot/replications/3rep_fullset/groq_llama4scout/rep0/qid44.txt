## Step 1: Understanding the Types of Triangles
- **Scalene**: All sides are of different lengths.
- **Isosceles**: At least two sides are of equal length.
- **Right-angled**: One angle is 90 degrees.
- **Equilateral**: All sides are of equal length.

## 2: Analyzing Triangle \( BCD \)
- Since \( BC = CD \) (as \( ABCD \) is a rectangle), triangle \( BCD \) has at least two sides of equal length, making it **isosceles**.
- The angle at \( C \) is 90 degrees because \( ABCD \) is a rectangle, making triangle \( BCD \) also **right-angled**.
