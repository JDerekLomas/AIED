## Step 1: Understand the concept of perpendicular lines in relation to the given line \(y=5\).
The line \(y=5\) is a horizontal line. For a line to be perpendicular to a horizontal line, it must be a vertical line. Vertical lines have equations of the form \(x = a\), where \(a\) is a constant.
