## Step 1: Understanding the Misconception
A common misconception is confusing the direction of the inequality or the type of circle (open or closed) to use. Students might mistakenly think that a closed circle (filled) is used for greater than or equal to (\(\geq\)), and an open circle for less than or greater than. However, the direction of the inequality (\(>\) or \(<\)) dictates the direction of the arrow.
