## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question asks for the mean shoe size given a table of shoe sizes and their frequencies. To calculate the mean, we need to find the total sum of the products of each shoe size and its frequency, then divide by the total frequency.

## 2: Calculating the Mean
First, calculate the total frequency: $5 + 8 + 9 + 5 + 3 = 30$.
Then, calculate the sum of the products of each shoe size and its frequency:
$(2 \times 5) + (3 \times 8) + (4 \times 9) + (5 \times 5) + (6 \times 3) = 10 + 24 + 36 + 25 + 18 = 113$.
The mean shoe size is $\frac{113}{30} \approx 3.77$.
