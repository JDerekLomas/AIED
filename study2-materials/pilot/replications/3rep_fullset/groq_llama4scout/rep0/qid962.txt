## 3: Solving the Question
Let's denote the total number of bikes as \(T\). Since blue bikes are 3/8 of \(T\) and white bikes are 1/8 of \(T\), we have:
\[ \frac{3}{8}T - \frac{1}{8}T = 30 \]
Simplifying:
\[ \frac{2}{8}T = 30 \]
\[ \frac{1}{4}T = 30 \]
\[ T = 30 \times 4 \]
\[ T = 120 \]
