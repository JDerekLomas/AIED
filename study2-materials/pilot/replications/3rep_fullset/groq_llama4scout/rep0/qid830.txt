## Step 1: Understanding the Question

## 2: Solving for \(y\)
To find \(y\), we substitute \(x = 0.3\) into the equation \(2x + 3y = 12\).
\[2(0.3) + 3y = 12\]
\[0.6 + 3y = 12\]
\[3y = 12 - 0.6\]
\[3y = 11.4\]
\[y = \frac{11.4}{3}\]
\[y = 3.8\]
