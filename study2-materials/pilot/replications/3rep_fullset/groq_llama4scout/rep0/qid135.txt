## Step 1: Understand the question
The question presents a diagram with a circle at the top containing 60%, and two circles below it, one with 2/5 and the other blank. The task is to find the missing value, implying that the sum of the percentages or proportions from the top circle is distributed to the two bottom circles.

## 2: Convert all values to the same format
To compare and calculate easily, convert all given values to decimals or percentages. The top circle has 60%, one bottom circle has 2/5 which is 40% (since 2/5 * 100 = 40%).

## 3: Calculate the missing value
If 60% is split into two portions, one of which is 40%, then the missing portion is 60% - 40% = 20%. To express 20% in different formats: as a fraction it's 1/5, and as a decimal it's 0.2.
