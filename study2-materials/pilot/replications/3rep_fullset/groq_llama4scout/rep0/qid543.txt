1. **Understanding Reflections**: A reflection across a line (in this case, a horizontal line) involves flipping the object over that line. For a point or an object, its image after reflection across a line is the same distance from the line but on the opposite side.

   - Not flipping the object (mirroring it) across the line of reflection.
   - Incorrectly determining the position of the reflected object relative to the line of reflection.
