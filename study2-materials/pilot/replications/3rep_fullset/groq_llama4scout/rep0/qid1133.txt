## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question presents a pictogram representing Bob's music collection, with different types of music and the number of CDs for each type. The task is to find out how many more Pop CDs Bob has than Rock CDs.

## 2: Interpreting the Pictogram
Each symbol in the pictogram represents 4 CDs. The Pop CDs are represented by 3 and a half symbols, and the Rock CDs by 2 symbols.

## 3: Calculating Pop and Rock CDs
- Pop CDs = \(3.5 \times 4 = 14\)
- Rock CDs = \(2 \times 4 = 8\)

## 4: Finding the Difference
To find how many more Pop CDs Bob has than Rock CDs, subtract the number of Rock CDs from the number of Pop CDs:
\(14 - 8 = 6\)
