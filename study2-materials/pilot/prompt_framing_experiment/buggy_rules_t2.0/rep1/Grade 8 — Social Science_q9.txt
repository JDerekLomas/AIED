1.  **Semantic Blur:** Confounding "Democracy" with "Independence/Freedom" (e.g., "It is necessary because we are free").
2.  **Functional Overgeneralization:** Attributing the role of the Executive to the Legislature (e.g., "Parliament builds roads and helps the poor").

5.  **Role Confusion:** Attributing the "representative" role only to the Prime Minister or President rather than the institution.

For many, the lack of exposure to "functioning" democratic spaces in daily life makes these concepts feel purely academic.
