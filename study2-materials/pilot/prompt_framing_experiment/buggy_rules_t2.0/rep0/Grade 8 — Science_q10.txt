1. "One-sided heating": The student focuses only on boiling the milk (common kitchen practice) and ignores the "sudden cooling" step, which is cognitively distinct from typical cooking.

Step 3: Target population considerations
The open-ended recall format is significantly harder for ELL (English Language Learner) students than multiple-choice. Students often recognize the term on a milk packet but lack the formal expressive vocabulary to describe the "rapid cooling" sequence.
