1.  **"Natural is Always Better" Bug:** A systematic belief (prevalent in agricultural contexts) that manure, being "natural/organic," always produces a "better" or "faster" plant than "poisonous/harmful" chemicals. This leads to selecting Pot A.
2.  **Qualitative vs. Quantitative Bug:** Focusing on health/sturdiness (Pot A) rather than the specific metric of "fast/tall" growth (Pot B).
