*   **LCM vs. HCF Confusion:** Students often see two numbers and automatically try to find the Lowest Common Multiple (LCM) because they are more accustomed to it from fraction addition. *Result: Incorrect.*
*   **Keyword Dependency:** A student might see "maximum" and correctly guess HCF without understanding why.
*   **Operation Error:** Thinking the "total" weight is needed and adding 75 + 69. *Result: Incorrect.*
