2. **The "Single Category" Bug:** Listing two vitamins or two minerals instead of one of each, failing the "AND" logical constraint in part (b).
3. **The "Vague Health" Bug:** Defining protective foods simply as "they are good for health" without the specific mechanism of disease prevention/fighting required by the rubric.

5. **The "Mismatched Pair" Bug:** Correctly naming a deficiency disease (e.g., Goitre) but attributing it to a Vitamin instead of a Mineral.
