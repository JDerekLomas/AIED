Step 2: Buggy Rules
- "Equal-Angle Bias": Assuming all unknown angles in a quadrilateral are equal to the given angle ($x = 100$).
- "Linear Pair Overgeneralization": Treating any two visible angles as supplementary regardless of their spatial relationship.
- "Language-Logic Gap": Finding the correct numerical value through intuition but failing the "reason/calculation" rubric requirement due to limited English proficiency.
- "Operation Reversal": Adding the given angle to 180 instead of subtracting.
