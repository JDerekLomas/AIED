- "The Great Man" fallacy (viewing colonial impact solely through individual leaders rather than systemic policies). This might lead to vague answers.
- Temporal confusion (mixing pre-colonial and post-colonial shifts). This would lead to an incorrect answer.
- Over-generalization (assuming all tribes were affected the same way). While a generalization, the rubric allows for this, provided it mentions disruption or loss of control.
