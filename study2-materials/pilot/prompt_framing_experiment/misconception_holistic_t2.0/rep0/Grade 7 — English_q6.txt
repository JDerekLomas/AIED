*   **Conflating content with style:** Students often describe *what* the poem is about (e.g., "nature") rather than *how* it is written (e.g., "descriptive"). This leads to an incorrect answer.

*   **Vocabulary limitations:** Students may understand the style but lack the English adjective to label it.
