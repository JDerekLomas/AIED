Step 2: List common misconceptions.
*   **Spontaneous Generation:** Belief that the patches "appear" out of nowhere due to age or chemical reaction rather than biological agents. (Incorrect answer: "The bread turned old," "The heat made it change.")
*   **Abiotic Decay:** Thinking changes are caused by external dirt or dust settling on the bread. (Incorrect answer: "Dust," "Dirt.")
*   **Moisture as the agent:** Mistaking the environment (moisture) for the cause itself. (Incorrect answer: "Water," "Humidity.")
