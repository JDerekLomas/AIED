Step 2: List the most common misconceptions.
*   **Weathering vs. Soil Formation:** Students may confuse the process (weathering) with a factor (climate). This would likely still be marked correct if they name a related factor.
*   **Water/Air as primary factors:** Many students associate soil with "mud and water" and might answer "Water," which is a medium of weathering but not always listed as a primary factor in NCERT textbooks. This could lead to an incorrect or borderline answer.
*   **Man-made intervention:** Some students might think "fertilizers" or "farming" creates soil. This would be incorrect.
