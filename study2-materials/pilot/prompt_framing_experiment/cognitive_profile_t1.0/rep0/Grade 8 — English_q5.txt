Given the linguistic barrier, many students may resort to copying a single line from the poem or focusing on the literal characters (the King, the Old Man) rather than the underlying message of liberty.

The student must retrieve vocabulary, construct grammatically correct sentences, and synthesize the poem’s meaning.
