King Ashoka was a very big and brave king in olden days India. After one big fight named Kalinga war, he feel very sad because too much blood and people died. 

Two things he do:
1. He stop fighting wars and he follow Lord Buddha. 
2. He wrote good things on big stones and pilars so people can read. He tell everyone to do "Dhamma" means respect elders and be kind. 

He is important because he change his heart and become peace king.
