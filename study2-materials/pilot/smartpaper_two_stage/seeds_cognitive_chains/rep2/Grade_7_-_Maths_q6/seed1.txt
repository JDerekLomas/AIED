first $1 \frac{1}{2}$ will be $\frac{3}{2}$ 

next $3 \frac{1}{4}$ multiply so $3 \times 4 = 12$ and add 1 is $13$. so $\frac{13}{4}$

now multiply $\frac{3}{2} \times \frac{13}{4}$ 

upper number is $3 \times 13 = 39$ 
down number is $2 \times 4 = 6$ 

answer is $39/6$
