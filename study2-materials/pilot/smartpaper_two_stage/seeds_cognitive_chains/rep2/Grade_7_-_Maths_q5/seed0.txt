Total value of paise:

1. 10 paise 10 coin = 10 $\times$ 10 = 100 paise
2. 5 coins 20 paise = 5 $\times$ 20 = 100 paise
3. 16 coins 50 paise = 16 $\times$ 50 = 800 paisa

Sum of paisa = 100 + 100 + 800 = 1000 paisa

We know 100 paisa is 1 rupee.
So 1000 paisa is 10 rupee.
