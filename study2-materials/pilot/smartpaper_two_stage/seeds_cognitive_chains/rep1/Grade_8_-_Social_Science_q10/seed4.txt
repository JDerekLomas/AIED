East India company come to India for do business and trade only. They want spice and cotton clothes. They making big building called factory near the river for keep things. Then king give them farman and they start fight with local nawabs because they not want pay tax. Then battle of Plassey happen in 1757 and Company win because Robert Clive was very clever. Now Company becomes the Diwan of Bengal and taking all the land money from farmers. Slowly slowly they use force and police and taking more territory like Tipu Sultan and Maratha and they becoming kings.
