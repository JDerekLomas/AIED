Total value of paise =
10 coin of 10 pise = 100 paise
5 coin of 20 pise = 100 paise
16 coin of 50 pise = 16 x 50 ... it is 800 paise.

Now we add all. 
100 + 100 + 800 = 1000 paise.

We know 100 paise = 1 rupye.
So 1000 paise = 10 rupye.

Question asking how many 5 rupye coin.
10 rupye / 5 = 2.

Ans - 2 coins of 5 rupye.
