4.  **Attempt but Fail at Description (Moderate Likelihood):**

    *   **Impact:** The rubric makes this less critical for *passing* (they just need to name two). If they name two and completely botch the description, they still get the point.

5.  **Focusing on one correct occupation, failing to name a second (High Likelihood):**
    *   Many students might know "farming" or "agriculture" but struggle to name another prominent rural occupation specific to Rajasthan. The rubric explicitly requires *two*.
