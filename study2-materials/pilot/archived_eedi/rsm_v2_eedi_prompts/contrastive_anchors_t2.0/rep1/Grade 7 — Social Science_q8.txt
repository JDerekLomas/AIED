* **Specific Knowledge of Rajasthan:** Knowledge of common economic activities prevalent in the rural areas of Rajasthan. This is a fairly common topic in Indian social science curricula at this level, as state-specific geography and economy are usually covered.

**Language Demands:**
* **Key terms:** "Main occupations," "rural areas," "Rajasthan," "describe in brief."
* **English Proficiency:** The question uses relatively simple and common English vocabulary. "Occupations" might be slightly less common than "jobs" but should be understandable in context. "Describe in brief" is a straightforward instruction.
* **Writing demands:** Students need to recall and list at least two occupations and then elaborate briefly on one. This requires basic sentence construction in English. **Type of Task:**
* **Recall:** Primarily recall of factual information about the common economic activities in rural Rajasthan.
* **Description:** A minor descriptive component, requiring a brief explanation of one chosen occupation. This is still largely knowledge-based, not complex reasoning or application.

**Difficulty Comparison to Textbook Exercises:**
* This question is **highly typical** of a recall-based question found in a Class 7 Social Science textbook or end-of-chapter exercises. It directly probes factual knowledge often emphasized when teaching about regional economies and livelihoods.
