*   **"What are the main occupations..."**: This part requires recall of specific information. Agriculture/farming is universally recognized as a primary rural occupation in India. Handicrafts might be less universally known as a *main* occupation for all rural areas, but it's valid.
*   **"...of people in the rural areas of Rajasthan?"**: This specifies the region. While general knowledge of rural India might apply, specifying Rajasthan adds a layer of precision.
*   **"Describe any one in brief."**: This part is *not* required for the rubric. The rubric only asks for *naming at least two*. This is a crucial distinction. If the rubric *required* a description, the pass rate would plummet even further.
