**Question:** "Write a short paragraph (4-5 sentences) about 'My Favourite Hobby'. Mention what it is, when you do it, and why you enjoy it."

**Key Challenges for Class 7 Indian Government School Students (economically weaker sections, limited English):**

1.  **"Write a short paragraph (4-5 sentences)":**
    *   **"Write":** Basic writing ability in English.
    *   **"Short paragraph":** Understanding what constitutes a paragraph (linked sentences, not just a list). Many may struggle with sentence structure and cohesion.
    *   **"(4-5 sentences)":** Maintaining sentence count can be difficult. Some will write less, some will write much more, not understanding the constraint.

2.  **"My Favourite Hobby":**
    *   **"Favourite":** Many students will know this word.
    *   **"Hobby":** This is a relatively common word, but some might confuse it with a "game" or "activity" without the leisure/regular aspect.
    *   **Generating an idea:** Most will have *something* they enjoy, but articulating it in English is the hurdle.

3.  **"Mention what it is, when you do it, and why you enjoy it."**
    *   **"Mention":** Understanding the prompt requires including *all three* specific details. This is where many will fail to meet the rubric fully.
    *   **"What it is":** Relatively straightforward.
    *   **"When you do it":** Requires time expressions (e.g., "in the evening," "on weekends," "after school"). This can be tricky.
    *   **"Why you enjoy it":** This demands expressing *reasons* and *feelings* in English. This is often the hardest part, moving beyond simple descriptions to explanations.
