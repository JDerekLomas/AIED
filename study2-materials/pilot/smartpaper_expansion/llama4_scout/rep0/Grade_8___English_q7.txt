## Step 1: Understanding the Question
The question asks students to identify rhyming words from a given stanza. The stanza provided is:
"Him shelter from rain,
And a mouthful of grain,
He wished only to borrow,
He had repay it tomorrow."
The task is to find rhyming words, specifically pairs that have the same ending sound.

## 2: Identifying Rhyming Words
The rhyming words in the stanza are 'rain' and 'grain'. The rubric for full marks requires students to correctly pair 'rain' with 'grain' as rhyming words.

## 3: Potential Errors or Misunderstandings
Common errors might include:
- Incorrectly identifying rhyming words (e.g., pairing 'borrow' with 'tomorrow', which actually do rhyme, but not focusing on 'rain' and 'grain').
- Failing to identify any rhyming words.
- Pairing words that do not rhyme.
