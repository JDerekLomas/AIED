The question asks students to explain why vitamins and minerals are called 'protective foods' and to name one disease caused by the deficiency of a vitamin and one by a mineral. ## 2: Identify prerequisite knowledge
Students need to have basic knowledge about the role of vitamins and minerals in the body, specifically that they are essential for health and can help prevent diseases.

- Failing to provide correct examples of diseases caused by deficiencies.
