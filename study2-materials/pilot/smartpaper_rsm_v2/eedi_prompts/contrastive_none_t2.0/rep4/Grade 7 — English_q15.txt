    *   **Translating Ideas:** Students are likely to think in Hindi (or their regional language) and attempt a mental translation. This can lead to grammatically incorrect or awkward English sentences, even if the underlying idea is correct.
    *   **Rubric Match:** Even if they express the hobby and when they do it, articulating "why they enjoy it" with sufficient clarity to satisfy the rubric can be a stumbling block due to linguistic limitations.
