*   **Categorization:** Implicitly, they need to understand what constitutes a "mountain range" to correctly identify one.

*   **"any two":** Clear and unambiguous.

*   **Recall:** This is almost entirely a recall-based question. Students are expected to have learned the names of major mountain ranges in India and retrieve them from memory. There's no application of concepts or complex reasoning involved.

*   **Similar Difficulty:** This question is very similar to a typical "fill in the blanks," "name the following," or "list two examples of" type of exercise found in Class 6 Social Science textbooks. It's a fundamental knowledge check.
