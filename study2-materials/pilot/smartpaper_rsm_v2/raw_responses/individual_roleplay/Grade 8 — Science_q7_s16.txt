हाँ, Gardener ko kuch din baad change dikhega.

**Reasons:**

1.  **Smell bad (badboo):** Pehle gandi smell aayegi because some things are rotting.
2.  **Size kam ho jayega:** Jo cheezein pit mein daali hain, unka size chota ho jayega. Pile (dher) kam ho jayega.
3.  **Color change:** Sab cheezein brown ya black ho jayengi.
4.  **Bugs/Worms:** Usmein chhote chhote keede (bugs) aur earthworms dikhenge.
5.  **Heat:** Agar haath lagayenge toh thoda garam feel ho sakta hai.

Ye sab changes microorganisms (jaise bacteria aur fungi) ki wajah se hote hain. Ye microorganisms plants, vegetables aur fruits ko todte hain (decompose karte hain).

**Is this beneficial for soil health?**

Yes, this is very beneficial for soil health.

**How it is beneficial:**

1.  **Makes good manure (khad):** Jo sad-gal kar banta hai, usko compost kehte hain. Ye bahut acchi khad hoti hai.
2.  **Increases fertility:** Ye khad soil ko zyada fertile banati hai, jisse plants acche ugte hain.
3.  **Adds nutrients:** Ismein saare zaroori nutrients hote hain
