**Main occupations in rural areas of Rajasthan are:**

1. **Agriculture (Kheti):** This is the most important one. Many people do farming. They grow crops like bajra, wheat, jowar, pulses (dal), and some spices.

2. **Animal Husbandry (Pashupalan):** People keep animals like cows, buffaloes, goats, sheep, and camels. They get milk, wool, and sometimes sell animals.

3. **Handicrafts:** Some people make things by hand like pottery (mitti ke bartan), weaving (kapde bunna), block printing (chhapayi), and making leather goods (chamde ka saman).

4. **Labour (Mazdoori):** Many people work as labourers in farms, construction, or other small jobs.

5. **Small Shops and Businesses:** Some people have small shops for daily needs, tea stalls, or provide services like repairing cycles.

**Description of Agriculture:**

Agriculture is the main job for most people in Rajasthan villages. Farmers work on their fields. They prepare the land, sow seeds, water the crops if there is a well or canal, and harvest them when they are ready. They use ploughs, tractors (if they have one), and other tools.
