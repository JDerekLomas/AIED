Harappan Civilization ka ek khaas baat unka shehar banane ka tareeka tha.

Unke shehar bahut achhe se banaye gaye the. Roads seedhi hoti thi aur ek doosre ko kaat-ti thi. Ghar bhi achhe se banaye jate the. Har ghar mein toilet or bathroom hota tha. Pani nikalne ke liye acchi naaliyan hoti thi. Ye sab unki planning ko bahut khaas banata tha.
