Harappan civilisation mein ek khaas baat unke shahar banane ka tareeka tha.

Unke shahar bahut achhe se plan kiye hue the. Roads sidhi hoti thi aur ek doosre ko 90 degree par kaat ti thi. Ghar kachhi aur pakki eeton se bane hote the. Har ghar mein toilet aur nahane ki jagah hoti thi. Shaharon mein ek bada naliyon ka system tha jo ganda pani bahar nikalta tha. Ye naliyan dhaki hui hoti thi.

Ye ek khaas feature tha.
