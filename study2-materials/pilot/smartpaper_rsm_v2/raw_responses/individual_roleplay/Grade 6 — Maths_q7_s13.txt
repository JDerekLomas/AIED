Morning 10 baje temperature = 30 degree C

Har 1 ghante mein temperature badhta hai = 2 degree C

10 baje se 4 baje tak kitne ghante honge?

10 baje se 11 baje = 1 ghanta
11 baje se 12 baje = 1 ghanta
12 baje se 1 baje = 1 ghanta
1 baje se 2 baje = 1 ghanta
2 baje se 3 baje = 1 ghanta
3 baje se 4 baje = 1 ghanta

Total ghante = 6 ghante

Temperature kitna badhega?
6 ghante mein = 6 * 2 degree C = 12 degree C

4 baje sham ko temperature kya hoga?
Pehle ka temperature + badha hua temperature
= 30 degree C + 12 degree C
= 42 degree C

Answer: Jodhpur ka temperature 4 baje sham ko 42 degree C hoga.
