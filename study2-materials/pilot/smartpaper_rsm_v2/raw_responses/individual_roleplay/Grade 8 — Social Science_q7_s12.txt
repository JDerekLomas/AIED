Battle of Plassey 1757 mein hua tha.

Is ladai se British bahut takatwar ho gaye India mein.

Siraj-ud-daulah Bengal ka nawab tha. Usse haraya gaya.

Mir Jafar ko nawab banaya gaya. Wo British ka puppet tha.

British ko bahut paisa mila. Bengal unke control mein aa gaya.

Phir unhone aur jagah bhi kabza karna shuru kar diya.

Isse unka raj badhne laga.

India mein British empire banana shuru ho gaya.
