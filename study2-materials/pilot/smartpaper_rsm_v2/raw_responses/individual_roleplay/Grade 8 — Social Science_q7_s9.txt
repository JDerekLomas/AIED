**Battle of Plassey, 1757**

Ye ladai bahut important thi. Isse Angrez India mein aur strong ho gaye.

**Kaise:**

1.  **Bengal pe control:** Plassey ke baad, Angrezon ka Bengal pe control ho gaya. Bengal bahut ameer province tha. Wahan se unko bahut paisa mila.

2.  **Puppet Nawab:** Unhone Mir Jafar ko Nawab bana diya. Wo unka kathputli tha. Angrez jo bolte the, Mir Jafar wahi karta tha. Isse unki taqat badhi.

3.  **Trade me fayda:** Unko trade mein bahut fayda mila. Tax kam ho gaye ya nahi lagte the unke saman pe. Doosre traders ko nuksan hua.

4.  **Military power:** Ladai jeetne se unki sena aur taqatwar lagne lagi. Doosre raja darne lage.

Isse Angrezon ka raj India mein badhna shuru ho gaya.
