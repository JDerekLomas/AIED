Harappan Civilization ka ek khaas baat unka shehar banane ka tareeka tha.

Unke shehar bahut achhe se bane hue the. Sadke seedhi hoti thi aur ek doosre ko kaat-ti thi. Ghar bhi ek line mein bane hote the. Har ghar mein nahane aur shauchalaya ka intazam hota tha. Pani nikalne ke liye acchi naaliyan bani thi. Ye sab unki planning ko bahut khaas banata hai.
