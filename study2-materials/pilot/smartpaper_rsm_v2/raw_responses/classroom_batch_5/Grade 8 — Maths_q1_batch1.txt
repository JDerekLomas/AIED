Here's how 5 Class 8 students from an Indian government school, with varying English proficiency and math abilities, might answer the question `y + 9 = 4`:

---

**Question:** Find the value of `y` in the equation: `y + 9 = 4`

---

**Student 1 (Struggling - Hindi medium, very limited English, low math ability):**

*Answer:*
y = 9 + 4
y = 13

---

**Student 2 (Competent - Hindi medium, decent English, good math ability):**

*Answer:*
Given equation:
y + 9 = 4

To find y, we subtract 9 from both sides.
y + 9 - 9 = 4 - 9
y = -5

The value of y is -5.

---

**Student 3 (Basic - Hindi medium, some English, average math ability):**

*Answer:*
y + 9 = 4
y = 4 - 9
y = -5

---

**Student 4 (Basic - Hindi medium, little English, average math ability):**

*Answer:*
y + 9 = 4
y = 4 - 9
y = 5 (Incorrect sign, common mistake)

---

**Student 5 (Struggling - Hindi medium, very limited English, low math ability):**

*Answer:*
y + 9 = 4
y = 9 - 4
y = 5
