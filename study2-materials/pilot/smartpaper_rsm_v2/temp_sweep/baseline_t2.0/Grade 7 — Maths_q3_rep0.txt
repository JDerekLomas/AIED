*   **Common Mistakes:** Very few, perhaps a rare arithmetic error or a momentary lapse in memory for the `1/2`.

**advanced:** **98%** full marks
*   **Language Barrier:** No issue here.
*   **Recall vs. Reasoning:** They will know the formula and apply it correctly without hesitation. This would be considered a very straightforward application question for them.
*   **Common Mistakes:** Extremely rare, perhaps a single student making a careless arithmetic error. They might even try to cross-check their answer in their head.
