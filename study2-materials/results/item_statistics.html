Statistical Limitations (Full Dataset)

  • 25% of items have n < 100 responses - target rate estimates are unstable
  • Items skew hard - median 88% incorrect (not representative of typical diagnostics)
  • 117x variation in response counts (41 to 4,825 per item)

Final Subset (36 items)

Based on psychometric filtering, we selected 36 high-quality items (35 unique questions) for the LLM experiment:

  • Excluded misconception 1214 - 97% initial mastery (P(L₀)), students rarely exhibit this
  • Discrimination > 0.5 - All items effectively distinguish ability levels
  • n > 100 responses - Stable human baseline estimates
  • Human target rate > 10% - Misconception actually observed

Final composition: 15 order-of-operations (1507), 13 negative multiplication (1597), 8 fraction addition (217)
