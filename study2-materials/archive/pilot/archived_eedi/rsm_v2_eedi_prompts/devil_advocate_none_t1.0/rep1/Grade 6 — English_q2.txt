    *   *Self-correction:* The difficulty isn't in understanding the *concept* of a fairy or living in a book, but in *retrieving* that specific detail from the (unseen) preceding text. If the text was simple and direct, this is a basic recall question.

3.  **Exam Anxiety/Blank Responses:**
    *   This is always a factor. Students might know the answer but get overwhelmed and just leave it blank or write something completely irrelevant.
    *   *Self-correction:* This contributes to a lower score than pure knowledge would suggest.
