**Analyzing the Question:**

*   **"A gardener has collected waste of plants, vegetables and fruits in a pit."** - This sets the context. "Waste," "plants," "vegetables," "fruits," "pit" are fairly common words, but "collected" might be less common than "put."
*   **"Does gardener notice any change after some days?"** - This is the core yes/no or descriptive part. "Change" is a key word. "Some days" implies a relatively short timeframe.
*   **"Give reasons..."** - Requires understanding *why* the change happens. This is a higher-order thinking skill.
*   **"...and evaluate if this is beneficial for soil health."** - Requires understanding the *impact* and forming a judgment. "Beneficial" and "soil health" are crucial vocabulary.

    *   The overall sentence structure is more complex than simple declarative sentences.
